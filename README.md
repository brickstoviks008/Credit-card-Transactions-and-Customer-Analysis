# Credit-card-Transactions-and-Customer-Analysis
📌 **PROJECT OVERVIEW**

This project focuses on analyzing credit card transactions and customer behavior using interactive Power BI dashboards. The objective of the project is to derive meaningful insights from credit card data to help financial institutions understand customer spending patterns, revenue generation, interest earnings, and customer demographics.

Two dashboards were created to analyze the data from different perspectives:

1️⃣ Credit Card Transactions Dashboard – focuses on transaction activity and revenue generation.

2️⃣ Credit Card Customer Dashboard – focuses on customer demographics, income, satisfaction, and behavior.

By visualizing key performance indicators and trends, the dashboards help identify high-value customers, spending categories, and business opportunities for credit card companies.

🎯 **PROJECT OBJECTIVES**:

The main objectives of this project are:

1.To analyze credit card revenue and transaction patterns
    2.To identify high-performing card categories

3.To understand customer spending behavior

4.To analyze customer demographics such as age, education, and job type

5.To measure customer satisfaction and interest earnings

6.To provide data-driven recommendations for business growth

🛠 **TOOLS USED**

• 📊 Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
• 📂 Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source
• 📂 Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
• 📝 Data Modeling – Relationships established among tables to enable cross-filtering and aggregation.
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs

📊 **DASHBOARD 1: CREDIT CARD TRANSACTION REPORT**

**KEY METRICS**:
• Total Revenue: 55.32M
• Total Interest Earned: 7.84M
• Total Transaction Amount: 45M
• Total Transactions:	656K

**Key Analysis**:
• Revenue by job type and age groupRevenue distribution by card category
• Transaction analysis by payment mode
• Quarterly revenue trends
• Spending patterns by category
• Revenue by job type and age group

🔎**Key Insights**:
1.**Card Category Performance**
• Blue cards generate the highest revenue (~46M).
• Silver cards contribute moderate revenue (~6M).
• Gold and Platinum cards contribute the least, indicating low adoption.
2.**Transaction Mode**
• Swipe payments dominate (35M).
• Chip transactions contribute 17M.
• Online transactions are very low (3M).
3.**Spending Categories**
Top spending areas include:
• Bills
• Entertainment
• Fuel
• Groceries
• Food
• Travel
4.**Customer Age Insights**
• Customers aged 40–50 generate the highest revenue.
• Younger customers contribute significantly less.

👥**DASHBOARD 2:CREDIT CARD CUSTOMER ANALYTICS**:

**Key Analysis**:
•  Customer satisfaction trends
•  Interest earned by age group
•  Transaction amount by marital status
•  Income distribution by job type
•  Customer demographic patterns

🔎**Key Insights**
**1.Customer Satisfaction**
• Overall Customer Satisfaction Score: 3.19 / 5.
• Satisfaction peaks among customers aged 40–50.
**2.Customer Demographics**
• Married customers contribute the highest transaction amounts (~50%).
• Business professionals have the highest income levels.
**3.Interest Earnings**
• Interest earnings peak around ages 45–50.
• After age 55, earnings gradually decline.

📈**Business Insights**

From the analysis, the following business insights were identified:
• Blue credit cards dominate the portfolio revenue.
• Middle-aged customers (40–50) are the most profitable segment.
• Business professionals and white-collar employees are high-value customers.
• Swipe transactions dominate over online payments.
• Customer satisfaction is moderate, indicating opportunities for improvement.

**🚀 Business Recommendations**

**1.Promote Premium Cards**
      Increase adoption of Gold and Platinum cards through rewards and incentives.
**2.Encourage Online Transactions**
      Provide cashback or loyalty rewards for digital payments.
**3.Target Younger Customers**
     Offer digital-friendly features for customers aged 20–30.
**4.Improve Customer Experience**
     Introduce personalized offers and better support services.
**5.Focus on High-Income Segments**
     Target business professionals and white-collar workers with premium benefits.
