# 💳 Credit Card Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that analyzes **credit card transactions and customer behavior**. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that help understand **revenue generation, customer spending patterns, demographic trends, and card usage behavior**.

The project contains **two analytical dashboards**:

- **Credit Card Transactions Dashboard** – focuses on transaction activity and revenue analysis.
- **Credit Card Customers Dashboard** – focuses on customer demographics, income distribution, and satisfaction levels.

These dashboards help financial institutions identify **high-value customers, popular spending categories, and opportunities to improve customer engagement and revenue growth.**

---
# 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze **credit card transaction patterns**
- Identify **revenue contribution by card type**
- Understand **customer spending behavior**
- Evaluate **customer demographics and income segments**
- Track **interest earnings and satisfaction trends**
- Provide **data-driven business insights**

---

# 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **📊Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualization  
- **📂Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **🧠DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated metrics and KPIs  
- **📝Excel / CSV Dataset** – Data source

### Key Analysis
- Revenue distribution by **card category**

- Spending patterns by **category**

---

## 🔎 Key Insights

### Card Category Performance
- **Blue cards generate the highest revenue (~46M)**.
- **Silver cards contribute moderate revenue (~6M)**.
- **Gold and Platinum cards contribute the least**, indicating low adoption.

### Transaction Mode
- **Swipe payments dominate (35M)**.
- Chip transactions contribute **17M**.
- **Online transactions are very low (3M)**.

### Spending Categories
Top spending areas include:
- Bills
- Entertainment
- Fuel
- Groceries
- Food
- Travel

### Customer Age Insights
- **Customers aged 40–50 generate the highest revenue.**
- Younger customers contribute significantly less.

| Total Revenue | 55.32M |

| Total Customer Income | 576M |
| Customer Satisfaction Score | 3.19 |

### Key Analysis
- Customer satisfaction trends
- Interest earned by age group
- Transaction amount by marital status
- Income distribution by job type
- Customer demographic patterns

---

## 🔎 Key Insights

### Customer Satisfaction
- Overall **Customer Satisfaction Score: 3.19 / 5**.
- Satisfaction peaks among **customers aged 40–50**.

### Customer Demographics
- **Married customers contribute the highest transaction amounts (~50%).**
- **Business professionals have the highest income levels.**

### Interest Earnings
- Interest earnings peak around **ages 45–50**.
- After age **55**, earnings gradually decline.

---

# 📈 Business Insights

From the analysis, the following business insights were identified:

- **Blue credit cards dominate the portfolio revenue.**
- **Middle-aged customers (40–50)** are the most profitable segment.
- **Business professionals and white-collar employees** are high-value customers.
- **Swipe transactions dominate over online payments.**
- **Customer satisfaction is moderate**, indicating opportunities for improvement.

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

### 4️⃣ Improve Customer Experience
